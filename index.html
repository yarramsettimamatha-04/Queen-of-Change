About Us

Queens Of Change Foundation is a women-led NGO dedicated to breaking the stigma around menstruation and empowering underprivileged women across India through dignity, education, and opportunities.

Through menstrual health awareness programs, sanitary pad distribution drives, and community outreach initiatives, the foundation works to improve the lives of women and girls in underserved communities.
